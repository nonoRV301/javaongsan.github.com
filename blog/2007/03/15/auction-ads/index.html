Auction Ads

Mar 15th, 2007

I started using Auction Ads today

You earn money with Auction Ads when your site’s visitors click on an Auction Ad listing and take an action on eBay you earn cash. Actions are defined as a Winning Bid, a Buy-it-Now or a confirmed user registration. Payments is send out the first of every month via PayPal.

4 simple steps to start making money with Auction Ads
1. Sign Up
Sign up to be an Auction Ads publisher.
2. Insert Our Code
Insert our ad code on your web site.
3. Place Ads On Your Site
We place auction ads targeted to your content on your site.
4. Make Money
Users buy products they’re interested in and you make money.

Whats interesting is the AuctionAds Affiliate Program

There is no need to sign up, the affiliate program is built right into the ads you display! If a user clicks on the “Ads by AuctionAds” that is displayed and signs up for their service you earn a bonus 2% of all revenue generated by that user for the 6 months. Theres a 30 day cookie on all referrals.
