How I Get Traffic to My Blog?

May 8th, 2007

Making money from blogging depends a lot on the traffic you get. The more traffic the more profitable your blog. The number one question on every blogger’s mind is “How to I get traffic to my blog?”

Here are some strategy I use. When you developed a good strategy for creating traffic, you get ever increasing traffic.

Before Starting your traffic campaign, you should have a way to measure your traffic performances. Try Google Analytics, its one of the best free tool.

  1. SEO, get you website listed in the search engines for specific keywords that relate to your website. Brush up your SEO Basics.
  2. Use PPC (Pay Per Click) such as Google Adwords.
  3. Free Traffic Exhanges. Try this: 
         1. [trafficswarm](http://www.trafficswarm.com/go.cgi?606508)
     2. [trafficroundup](http://www.trafficroundup.com/newuser.php?ref=javaongsan)
     3. [trafficpods](http://www.trafficpods.com/index.jsp?ref=javaongsan/)
     4. [topsurfer](http://www.blogger.com/post-edit.g?blogID=22591662&postID=6460049186926221566#)
     5. [hitpulse](http://www.blogger.com/post-edit.g?blogID=22591662&postID=6460049186926221566#)
  4. Post on forums similar to your niche, and have a link in your forum signature file.
  5. Email to your friends tell them about your blog
  6. Recycle your blog post as articles and have them posted on Article Directories.
  7. Some good article directories to get you started: 
           1. [www.eZineArticles.com](http://www.ezinearticles.com/)
       2. [www.ultimatearticledirectory.com](http://www.ultimatearticledirectory.com/)
       3. [www.submityourarticle.com](http://www.submityourarticle.com/)
  8. Link Exchange from other sites.
  9. Create links to your blog from web 2.0 social networking sites.
  10. blog and ping. Basically, when you update your blog, you can let blog portals such as technorati.com know by pinging them. You can ping a lot of portals at once by using pingomatic.com.
  11. Interactions Between with other Blogs.

Try something every day that will help increase the traffic to your blog.
