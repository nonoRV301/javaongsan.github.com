Using Mybloglog to Drive Traffic to Your Blog

May 17th, 2007

If look closely on the left sidebar, you notice a widget name “mybloglog recent visitor”. Mybloglog is a web 2.0 blog social network. Its free and you get to lost all your blogs. Any member who likes your blog would be
able to join your blog community.

To draw visitors, you visit their blogs and leave comment on their community. However, I believe the success rate is very low. In order to be successful on mybloglog, you need a decent photo of yourself, not an object or cartoon, no studio photo as well. A good user name no weird, funny or sleazy ones, nothing associated with money. Next you start checking out mybloglog members and their blog. I run through at least a hundred of them per day. Most of them will start visiting your blog and community. There’s
nothing more exciting than getting new faces on the widget everyday.

The other great thing is that mybloglog has a statistical link for blog, which monitors your stats. There’s a widget that ranks the top 5 most clicked link. You be able to see where your visitors are clicking on. I use
this to change my affliate products on my blog. I put up a link to a product test it for 3 days, if its good it stays, if not I try another.

mybloglog is own by yahoo, and they have a lot of potential. Another features of mybloglog is that if you visit a blog for a number of time, you be added to their community, you can use thos features to get
more members.
