Making Money From Blogging #Earnings May

May 30th, 2007

Its almost the end of the month, I decided to declare all my blog earnings early as I do not expect much changes.

payperpost $74.51
bloggerwave $30
sponsoredreviews Amount $9.75
adsense $18.02
AdBrite $0.34
AuctionAds $2.05
suqidoo $2.36
Affiliate Sales $27

Total is $164

payperpost is my top income source, its also breaks down this way.
Amount To Be Paid for Post $44.51
Amount To Be Paid for Referrals: $15.00
Amount To Be Paid for ‘Review My Posts’: $15.00
So you not only make money from posting, but from referrals and reviews as well.

Not bad since I only take it seriously in May. My site hits average around 30 hits a day and highest day is only 54 hits. With 907 New Visits and 1,481 Pageviews in May, you can see that I really could make money from low traffic volume and is the reason why my adsense income is low. For my free traffic secret take a look at How I get traffic to my blog?.

Top post are How I get traffic to my blog? 48 hits and Making Money from Squidoo 47 hits.

I expect My income to grow exponentially when my PR and traffic goes up. My Goal for June is to make it pass $1000. Think I can do it?
