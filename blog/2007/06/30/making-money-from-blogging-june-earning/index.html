Making Money From Blogging #June Earning

Jun 30th, 2007

Its the end of the month and I am reporting my earning. I have not meet my target of $1000. As you can see the overall increase was from payperpost, but the rest source of income have dipped.

Source May June

payperpost $74.51 $146.12

sponsoredreviews $9.75 $16

[AdSense] $18.02 $16.07

AdBrite $0.34 $0.32

AuctionAds $2.05 $0.39

suqidoo $2.36 $1.82

blogitive 0 $5

bloggerwave $30 0

Affiliate Sales $27 $94

Total $164 $279.60

The drop are mainly because blogger mark my blog as spam at the beginning of the month for about 3 -4 days, and I remove most of the ad networks, change my template and did less paid posts. For next month, I will remove Adsense as well.
