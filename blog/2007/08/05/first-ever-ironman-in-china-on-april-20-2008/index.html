First-ever Ironman in China on April 20, 2008

Aug 5th, 2007

The first-ever Ironman in China will take place on April 20, 2008 in Haikou, the capital city of Hainan Province. According to ironmanlive.com Ironman China Ltd. (ICL) has announced that it has entered a hosting agreement with the City of Haikou, Hainan, for the staging of the inaugural Ironman China triathlon.

Hainan Island, known as the Hawaii of China, is a beautiful tropical island located in the South China Sea about an hour by air from Hong Kong.
