Common Blogging Questions

Aug 17th, 2007

Some of the very common questions in blogging are:

  1. How to get traffic to my blog?
  2. How often should I post?
  3. when should I ping my blog? Somehow all this question are directly or indirectly related to your blog traffic. What you writing and the frequency of your posting affects your traffic, whether you need to ping your blog. If you post once a day, your traffic is pretty much stable and if your articles are good, you have a steady base of readers. Google indexes your blog fast. If you are posting more than once a day, with some allowances apart, your blog will be mark to be index faster than someone who does it once in a few days.

If you have already burn your feed with feedburner, you be able to set up pingautomatic there too. Once you ave post feedburner pings your blog to a wide variety of rss readers. If your blog is new or if you do not update them regularly you need to ping it manually after each post.

To get more traffic to your blog, a simple solution is to post more than once a day. If you are able to post 4 times a day, you get a short charge of traffic instantly. Blog regularly and you get all the traffic you need.
