Learn How to Tumble Turn!

Sep 24th, 2007

This is possible coolest trick for a triathlete. It makes us look more pro, cool and its a lot of fun. I have only master tumble and working on the turn. Not bad for someone who is only swimming his second year.

How the tumble turn is done.
When almost reaching the wall forward somersault just before the wall. Body facing up, both feet planted on the wall, knees bent, hands overhead, stretched out. Push off with you feet. Twist your body back to front crawl position.

Breaking it up.

  • Somersault - Learn to somersault in the water. Breathe out and perform the somersault. Ending position facing up

  • Somersault and push-off on back - When you comfortable in the water, add the wall push off.

  • Add the turn - Now you need to know how to twist the body back to the front.

  • Put it all together - OK when you ready to put it all together. Well there’s not correct way for the tumble turn, what we want to achieve is minimizing time touch the wall and pushing off the wall with ours hands and hopefully shed a few seconds off our practice swims.
