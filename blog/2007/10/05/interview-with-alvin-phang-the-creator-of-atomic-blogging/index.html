Interview With Alvin Phang, the Creator of Atomic Blogging

Oct 5th, 2007

This week, the coder caught up with Alvin Phang, the creator of Atomic Blogging for quick 15 minutes interview. Why 15 minutes, thats the average attention span of blog readers.

Q. First off - can you tell us a little about yourself?
A. Sure. My name is Alvin Phang, 25 years old, a full time Internet Marketer & Blogger, creator of Atomic Blogging.

Q.When did start blogging? When did you become successful?
A.I started my blog since Oct 2006 at www.GatherSuccess.com. Its a blog about my experiences in making money online. What started as a hobby slowly become much popular after sharing of much
resources on how I gather people to my blog and started to generate some real income from my blogs.

Q.How frequently do you post?
A.Once a day every day.

Q.What kind of traffic does your blog get?
A.I get traffic from search engines, link backs from other blogs, social networks and much more.

Q.How much do you make from blogging? How did do it?
A.I make on the average 5 figures from blogging and how I basically do it is I provide quality content by simply sharing about real information to help others to succeed online.

Q.What is the biggest mistake people are making with blogging?
A.They are no serious in it and use free networks like blogger to start with, to seriously make money from blogging you got to use a hosted wordpress instead.

Q.What would you recommend that someone to just Starting Out?
A.If you are new to blogging I would recommend you can start of by getting Atomic Blogging package.As it comes with lots of step by step detail on how you can get started quickly with a profitable blog.

Q.Do you have any special offers for my readers?
A.There are already lots of special bonuses included already =)

Q.Okay…Thanks for your time Alvin. Do you have any last words of advice for people getting started?
A.Yes of course! Check out my blog www.GatherSuccess.com you will learn a lot there totally free of charge.
