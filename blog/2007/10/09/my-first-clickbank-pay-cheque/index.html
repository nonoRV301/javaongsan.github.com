My First Clickbank Pay Cheque

Oct 9th, 2007

I have just received my clickbank pay cheque of USD $37. I only cash out because my account had been inactive for a while. I have not been actively promoting affiliate programs and beside I preferred paypal.

Clickbank Payment Basics
The “Payment Threshold” setting in the clickbank account allows you to control when you receive a check. You may select an amount from $10 to $10,000 as your Payment Threshold. The default setting is $100. Your check is hold until your payable balance reaches the amount you select.

Why is my balance decreasing?
Accounts with a positive balance but no earnings for an extended period of time are considered dormant. Dormant accounts are subject to a charge of $1 per pay period after 90 days of inactivity, $5 per pay period after 180 days of inactivity, and $15 per pay period after 365 days of inactivity.

Bank charges
Handling commission : 1/8% of cheque amount subject to a minimum ofSGD10.00 and maximum of SGD100.00 Plus postage charge of SGD4.00
Overseas agent bank charge (differs, depending on the currency type andamount) USD5.00.
Thats a lot of money wasted and I have wait 3-4weeks.

Referral Program
The only product I wish to promote is the Referral Program.
Clickbank standard activation charge is $49.95, and you keep 20% of that, so you earn $9.99 up front.

Clickbank typical markup is $1 + 7.5%, and you keep 5% of that, so you earn about $0.09 to $0.99 on every sale.
