How to Make $100 a Day

Jan 25th, 2008

Quick and dirty way to make $100 a day.

  1. Use Go Daddy Bulk Domain Registration and grad 100 domains.

  2. Get a host, VPS prefer, from GoDaddy.com Hosting Plans or somewhere better.

  3. Download install wordpress with plugging like adsense-deluxe.php, google-sitemap-generator, and feedwordpress on all 100 domains.

  4. Setup rss feeds with feedwordpress. I prefer to use technorati search.

  5. Slap on Adsense.

  6. Submit sitemap to Google webmaster central.

  7. Go Live

Almost immediate, you start getting organic traffic from search engines. Adsense earning will start going up eventually will float around $1 after a week for each domain. For 100 domains, you make $100 a day. Its get more stable after a month and die off after that. Before that you could get 1 or 2 months of good organic traffic with almost 1000 uniques a day on 1 technorati tags.

If we work it out.

$1 x 100 (domains) x 60 (2 months) = $6000 - $700 (domain cost) - $50 (hosting) = $5 250.

Of course there several , downside to this techniques beside ethical issues. The original content owner will be unhappy and threaten you to remove their content. You also have a bandwidth issue, if you hosting bandwidth is too small, you site crash frequently. You also have redesign these 100 domains after they loose steam. Sometimes a website can die prematurely after a few days if you are not well verse in SEO.
