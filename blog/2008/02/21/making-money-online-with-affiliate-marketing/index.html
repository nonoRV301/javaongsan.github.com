Making Money Online With Affiliate Marketing

Feb 21st, 2008

To be an online business entrepreneur, one of the easiest ways to get started is with affiliate marketing. Affiliate marketing is to sell other peoples products and services and receive money. As an affiliate marketer, you sell either an advertiser’s product. You sign up with the company, are given a text link and usually nowadays, a host of graphics, banners and articles that relate to the company/product and you then have to promote it.

Affiliate marketing is one of the best income opportunities for several reason:

  • You Don’t Need Your Own Product

  • You don’t need to spend time creating a technically beautiful website

  • You don’t need to spend big bucks on great sales copy

  • You don’t need employees, or inventory, or any of the things that make a “business” a pain in the neck.

Does sound easy doesn’t it? Still many have fail, why? Affiliate marketing market is dynamic and constantly evolving. You need to keep pace to profits. The basics of affiliate marketing are Profitable Market, Products, Landing Page, Massive Traffic but these is only the basics. I have been studying successful marketers, most of them took an average of 6 months to figure out a successful campaign, they promote a minimum of 5 products in different niche, they spent up to $300 in pay-per-click search engine, they wrote tons of articles. One of the best affiliate marketers tutorial I have come across is Ewen Chia’s Autopilot Profits.

Ewen Chia’s Autopilot Profits really is a step by step, blue print for affiliate marketing and earning money online. If you are starting out this tutorial for you. Aim at internet marketing newbies Autopilot Profits consists of 6 video tutorials which can be watched online or downloaded, an Autopilot Profits eBook in PDF format, an Advertising eBook in PDF format and keywords / keyword phrases for use in your PPC campaigns.

If you download the videos you can always review them any time a question arises about a particular topic covered in the Autopilot Profits tutorial.
