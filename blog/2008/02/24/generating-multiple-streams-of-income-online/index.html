Generating Multiple Streams of Income Online

Feb 24th, 2008

Do you have only one website thats is bringing the cash now? Do you find it hard to increase the income from that site? If so, it may be time to set up another site theres complete different from what you are doing. Lets look at the types of sites we can set up.

Forum
Forum are the best source of passive income online, they require minimum management and most of the information are user contributed. The toughest thing about starting a forum is getting users to sign up and generate interesting topics. Revenue comes from private ad placement and ad networks.

Blog
Blog are the simplest to start. You write whatever you want. It the easy to get traffic. Revenue comes from a wide variety and is the most versatile of all.

Auto Blog
Its a blog that updates it self. A very effective way to make instant income but die as soon as it start making good profits.

Scripted Sites
Scripted Sites like myspace resources sites requires no maintainences makes primary income from Adsense. This site requires you to promote the site at the beginning.

These are some of the sites I have been playing with now. Whats are the type of sites you own?
