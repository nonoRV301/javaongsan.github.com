Google Adwords

Jun 11th, 2008

I been reading “The ultimate guide to google adwords by perry marshall and bryan todd.” It’s really good, if you are using Google AdWords or are planning to I high recommend you getting this. You get some bonus tools with this tools. A few mp3 which are really good. If you are not familiar with AdWords and think that you can just figure it out, I lost around $300 on AdWords doing that. So getting the book is a lot cheaper. I have to agreed I knew most of the stuff in the book but it really organizing all this knowledge into a system. I have started running a campaign on Yahoo Search Marketing. Sign up and get a $25 credit.. You can get $25 credit for every new account you sign up but you will need a new credit card for each account. I have not set up my new Adword account since I still waiting for new credit card to arrive (I have cancel all my cards previously). The plan is to learn to make $10 a day. After some experiment, I will looking forward to join Perry Marshall’s AdWords Consultation & Monthly Membership.

For those of you working on keywords for your AdWords , Wordtracker recently launched a promotion offering new subscribers unlimited 7 day access to the full version for FREE.
