Evil Way to Use Comments

Aug 15th, 2007

Not getting attention from top blogger in your niche? Here’s an very evil way to get attention from top bloggers. We all know you can post comment on other blogs but how about commenting for others on your own blog.

What you need? Name, Email, Blog URL. All these can be found on the targeted blog. Now put in a comment for all your favorite top bloggers, using their credentials. Put in something “This is an awesome post, I been watching you for a well, I think your are the best blogger around!”. Then go to the blogger that you have commented for and leave a comment “Thanks for coming by, I didn’t know you like my blog that much”. Do this with 20 top bloggers and you create an instant buzz to your blog and grad some attention that you been long craving for.

Increase Your Backlinks, Increase Your Page Rank

Aug 9th, 2007

It’s simple Google PageRank PR is directly related to the no. of backlinks to your blog. Not just any backlinks, links that will be pickup by google. To check your backlinks, go to google.com type in the search box “link:http://www.yoursite.com”. Before you exchange your links, you should check out the site that you are exchange links with.

What makes a good site for link exchange.?

  1. Site with a PR higher than yours is good.
  2. Site with lots of original contents.
  3. Site with at least 50 google links.
  4. Site with a large audiences contributions.
  5. Site with operated with a goal. Well this is not a checklist, you should be careful of were you linking to. It might seen difficult to exchange links with a larger, more successful site but if you are serious about your own site (operating your site with a goal) you will be able to get attention from them for your future growth. Link exchange should be treated with equal important for a website, as gathering content, traffic and design. Avoid link farm, exchange directories, or automated software.

Adding a Recent Comment Sidebar Widget for Blogger

Aug 5th, 2007

Many blog utilize the recent comment widget to encourage readers to leave comments on the blog.

To add a recent comment for your blogger blog. You need your comment rss feed which is “http://wizcoder.blogspot.com/feeds/comments/default”. Replace “wizcoder.blogspot.com” with your own blog url.

Then go to template page on blogger to “add a page element”, select rss feed, when ask for the feed url, fill in the comments url that you have already obtained. Add this to your sidebar, your recent comment will now appear here.

4 Blogging Mistakes

Aug 5th, 2007

Are you doing it all wrong? You may be killing your own blog.

You log in to your blog, you write the post, you logout. Standard routine. STOP! Think you are doing everything correctly? Don’t write another blog post until you read this.

Not Choosing the right title for your post
The title is the first contact point between you and your blog readers, if it fails to impact the reader, your post might not be read. How many times have simply chucking the title after you have complete writing the content? Its common for top bloggers to use interesting titles to catch the readers attention. Why spent a little more time on your titles.

Not using the right targeted keyword for your post
Every webmaster knows that keyword is the key to SEO. Choosing the right target keywords can make or break a website, influences its ranking on searching engines, but do you know that your content should have targeted keywords? How much time did you spent on planning that keyword for your content? Not only is the keyword for your content important, it is essential to tag your post with the right keyword tag.

Announcing your post
I have read from many top bloggers that you should email other blogger about your new post. I have not personally tried this and I probable should be doing. Ever wonder why some blogger’s post get mention by a few bloggers? Why not try emailing a few other bloggers for your next post asking for feedbacks.

Returning comments
I only learn about returning comments recently. One of the key ingredient of building a great blog is relationship building. Returning comments not only keeps the commenter on your blog but make your blog interactive, and thats the whole point of web 2.0 and blogging.

If you are making the same mistakes, STOP!

Suqidoo Introduces Featured Lenses and Work-in-Progress Lenses

Jul 25th, 2007

Suqidoo have split lens into 2 categories: Featured lenses and Work-in-Progress lenses. Featured lenses are those with a LensRank above a certain threshold. If your lens is featured, it shows up in Squidoo search results. Work-in-Progress lenses, on the other hand, have a LensRank below that threshold. If your lens is a Work-in-Progress, it won’t be featured in Squidoo search results. It will still be accessible via your direct URL and through other search engines. Featured lenses are marked with green check mark and and not Work-in-Progress lenses with gray minus sign. To get Featured lenses status you need to update your content and keep your rss feeds, guestbooks, plexus active.

What Can You Learn From Your Rss Reader?

Jul 24th, 2007

Do you us a rss reader to read all your favorite blogs? I use Thunderbird for my rss reader, simply because its my email reader.
There are somethings that we can learn from observing our rss reader to make our blog stand out from all the thousand of feed subscribed.

  1. Title
    Title are mostly alphabetically ranked so having a higher alphabet blog title helps. “A Way To Make Money Blogging” is higher than “Make Money Blogging”. Number is higher than alphabets.

  2. Interesting title for your post
    Reader are most likely to scan through all available blog post titles and pick the title they are interested in to read.

  3. A brief introduction to your post.
    f your post is long you might want to do a brief introduction paragraph to engage the readers into the main article or you can keep your post short to the point.

  4. Add a link back to your blog.
    Most rss feeds do not have a link back to their blog, or maybe some rss readers do not have that capabilities. You can simply add a link back to your blog at the end of your feed.

Locating a Niche Market

Jul 18th, 2007

The single most important task before starting a money making blog is to research a profitable niche to go into. Still many people tend to skip this step. Do not rush off to the hottest market out there and try to get a piece of the action. The idea of researching for a niche market is to find a hot market that have a large demand and very little supply, thus you dominate the market.

How are we going to locate that niche?

Take Inventory of your skills, hobbies, expertise. What are you good at? What do people come to you for advice?
Some sources of inspiration for us include:
* our hobbies, and the hobbies of our family or friends
* our work
* our personal interests and problems
* magazines and newspapers (I always get ideas from the Virgin airlines in-flight mag, Voyeur)
* TV shows, especially current affairs or niche channels (like Lifestyle or Discovery).

A great tool to help you research what people are searching online, on Google, msn, yahoo, etc is WordTraker. Do keyword research at http://www.wordtracker.com. Wordtracker offers a 2 hours free trial, however, you limited to the smallest search engine. In order to get Google results you can opt to purchase a one day license.

Find the highest KEI keyword. You should look at keywords with KEI of 10 and above, this indicates that market are not saturated. Examples of good keywords are Learn, tips, tutorial, instruction, buy, training. Try to narrow down the demographic of your niche. Dog is a wide nice, small dog is better target but something like healthy food for small dog will probably get you more targeted traffics.

Forum are another area to research niches. Look at forum headers on domz.org. Google for common headline, look at what people are asking. Find that right niche is the key to your success and you should be spending a large amount of time there.
