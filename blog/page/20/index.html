What Can You Learn From Your Rss Reader?

Jul 24th, 2007

Do you us a rss reader to read all your favorite blogs? I use Thunderbird for my rss reader, simply because its my email reader.
There are somethings that we can learn from observing our rss reader to make our blog stand out from all the thousand of feed subscribed.

  1. Title
    Title are mostly alphabetically ranked so having a higher alphabet blog title helps. “A Way To Make Money Blogging” is higher than “Make Money Blogging”. Number is higher than alphabets.

  2. Interesting title for your post
    Reader are most likely to scan through all available blog post titles and pick the title they are interested in to read.

  3. A brief introduction to your post.
    f your post is long you might want to do a brief introduction paragraph to engage the readers into the main article or you can keep your post short to the point.

  4. Add a link back to your blog.
    Most rss feeds do not have a link back to their blog, or maybe some rss readers do not have that capabilities. You can simply add a link back to your blog at the end of your feed.

Locating a Niche Market

Jul 18th, 2007

The single most important task before starting a money making blog is to research a profitable niche to go into. Still many people tend to skip this step. Do not rush off to the hottest market out there and try to get a piece of the action. The idea of researching for a niche market is to find a hot market that have a large demand and very little supply, thus you dominate the market.

How are we going to locate that niche?

Take Inventory of your skills, hobbies, expertise. What are you good at? What do people come to you for advice?
Some sources of inspiration for us include:
* our hobbies, and the hobbies of our family or friends
* our work
* our personal interests and problems
* magazines and newspapers (I always get ideas from the Virgin airlines in-flight mag, Voyeur)
* TV shows, especially current affairs or niche channels (like Lifestyle or Discovery).

A great tool to help you research what people are searching online, on Google, msn, yahoo, etc is WordTraker. Do keyword research at http://www.wordtracker.com. Wordtracker offers a 2 hours free trial, however, you limited to the smallest search engine. In order to get Google results you can opt to purchase a one day license.

Find the highest KEI keyword. You should look at keywords with KEI of 10 and above, this indicates that market are not saturated. Examples of good keywords are Learn, tips, tutorial, instruction, buy, training. Try to narrow down the demographic of your niche. Dog is a wide nice, small dog is better target but something like healthy food for small dog will probably get you more targeted traffics.

Forum are another area to research niches. Look at forum headers on domz.org. Google for common headline, look at what people are asking. Find that right niche is the key to your success and you should be spending a large amount of time there.

How to Make Money Online With Squidoo

Jun 17th, 2007

I just log on Squidoo and found that some of the interfaces have changed. Its more compact design now, I am able to view all my lens better now. Besides physical changes, Squidoo have achieve a lot since I last wrote about them. For instances, they hit 9 million page views per month, reached 150,000 lenses.

What is Squidoo?
Squidoo is an easy-to-build, single web page that can point to blogs, favorite links, RSS feeds, Flickr photos, Google maps, your eBay auctions, CafePress designs, Amazon books or music, and more. Squidoo offers highly focused content which often rises to the top of the search engine rankings very quickly. Squidoo has a PR7 (Page Rank) in Google. According to Alexa, Squidoo is ranked in the top 1000 sites on the whole web. So Squidoo is really a powerful site for you to get exposure. You might want to use this to promote your new site or blog but I am going to talk about making money directly from it.

First there a few terms you need to get use to.
1. Lens
It’s an easy-to-build, single web page that can point to blogs, favorite links, RSS feeds, Flickr photos, Google maps, your eBay auctions, CafePress designs, Amazon books or music, and more.

  1. Module
    Modules are the building blocks of lenses. A module makes it easy to point to links, images, blogs or even things to buy. Modules can be content with html or rss feeds or commerce-oriented.

You can view a sample of my lens: http://www.squidoo.com/automatedtrafficmachine/

How to make money?
You make money when visitors stops by your lens and buys a product you recommended or clicks on a Google ad, you get 50% of the royalties. If you don’t like money, select one of our 80+ nonprofit partners to donate these royalties to.

They mainly 2 types of payout here:
AdSense
This money goes into a pool, half of the pool distributes out to lensmasters, based on each lens’s average LensRank and traffic.

Affiliate revenue (ie, referral income–like Amazon and eBay and CafePress and SuperStore sales).
This is directly attributable to the lens that generated the revenue. The lensmaster gets a straight 50%.
Example, If your lens sells 1 book and Amazon pays us $1.05 in affiliate income, we send a nickel to charity and fifty cents to you.

Estimated earnings are shown on your MyLenses page you can view them everytime you logon.

Squidoo also have an referral scheme. Refer a friend to be a Squidoo lensmaster, when your friend’s lens earns its first $15, you AND your friend will each get $5, free!

How to build a successful lens?
1. Constant content updates
This can be easily done by including your blog’s rss feed to your lens. That way your lens is powered by your blog.
2. Join groups
There are very active groups that your lens can join within a smiliar niche
3. Use Plexo
Links, Amazon, Youtube and many more modules can be add as plexo. This allows other visitor to add their own links and vote their links up the ladder.
4. Traffic
The more traffic the more money.

This are some simple ways to build a lens. If you really want to be successful in Squidoo consider learning from one of the top Squidoo master around, Bob Jenkins. Squidoo Secrets
 are price at $47.

The basic idea of Squidoo is to make a many lens as possible, there no limits to the lens you can create. If you make $0.50 form 1 lens imagine what you have with 100 ens. The most important part is that its free, so create you Squidoo account now .
