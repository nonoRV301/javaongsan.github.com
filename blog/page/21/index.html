Using Mybloglog to Drive Traffic to Your Blog

May 17th, 2007

If look closely on the left sidebar, you notice a widget name “mybloglog recent visitor”. Mybloglog is a web 2.0 blog social network. Its free and you get to lost all your blogs. Any member who likes your blog would be
able to join your blog community.

To draw visitors, you visit their blogs and leave comment on their community. However, I believe the success rate is very low. In order to be successful on mybloglog, you need a decent photo of yourself, not an object or cartoon, no studio photo as well. A good user name no weird, funny or sleazy ones, nothing associated with money. Next you start checking out mybloglog members and their blog. I run through at least a hundred of them per day. Most of them will start visiting your blog and community. There’s
nothing more exciting than getting new faces on the widget everyday.

The other great thing is that mybloglog has a statistical link for blog, which monitors your stats. There’s a widget that ranks the top 5 most clicked link. You be able to see where your visitors are clicking on. I use
this to change my affliate products on my blog. I put up a link to a product test it for 3 days, if its good it stays, if not I try another.

mybloglog is own by yahoo, and they have a lot of potential. Another features of mybloglog is that if you visit a blog for a number of time, you be added to their community, you can use thos features to get
more members.

Blog Layout, Which Is the Best Template?

May 13th, 2007

Blog template are the most crucial piece of the parcel. Like a person, the
first impression is important, most visitors like myself jude a blog by its
layout.

Most template from wordpress, blogger, or any other platform is too common.
When I come across with same template, I might mistaken it as a blog I seen
before. Most header are too big, it fills up 80% of my browser. When I am
lazy to scroll, I might just leave the site.

Investing time to create your own layout definitely separate yourself from
others. A few points I taken from other and my blog. Keep your header small
with no ads near it, top or bottom. The more crowded the layout the more
popular. Regardless of what web design classes have thought us, it seem
that a less clean layout enjoys more success and makes more money. I have
more success with this template than any cleaner ones that I used. Loading
time is a consent for me, test ads and widgets, make sure there are not
taking up most the bandwidth. Only used light weight widgets and ad
networks.

The most important task is testing. Always make it a point to test and
measure, that’s the best way to find the most profitable template.

How I Get Traffic to My Blog?

Here are some strategy I use. When you developed a good strategy for creating traffic, you get ever increasing traffic.

Before Starting your traffic campaign, you should have a way to measure your traffic performances. Try Google Analytics, its one of the best free tool.

  1. SEO, get you website listed in the search engines for specific keywords that relate to your website. Brush up your SEO Basics.
  2. Use PPC (Pay Per Click) such as Google Adwords.
  3. Free Traffic Exhanges. Try this:

I tried to post from my 02, the basic functions are there, however the publishing button does not response. The only blogging mobile client available are only for typepad. Wordpress has a interface special for mobile devices.
