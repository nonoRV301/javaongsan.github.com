Its All About SYSTEM

Dec 1st, 2006

In any business, if you are looking for passive income, you should create a system, fine tune it, and perfect it, it will serve as a long flowing pipeline of cashflow. A system should allow a business owner to take 6 months or more off and return to a more the profitable business.

What is a good system for a Internet Marketing Business?

A Simple System that is duplicable
Set your Goal. Specific what do you want to accomplish by when.
Use Project Management to control time line.

  1. Locate a niche market
    Take Inventory of your skills, hobbies, expertise. What are you good at? What do people come to you for advice?
    Some sources of inspiration for us include:
  2. our hobbies, and the hobbies of our family or friends
  3. our work
  4. our personal interests and problems
  5. magazines and newspapers (I always get ideas from the Virgin
    airlines in-flight mag, Voyeur)
  6. TV shows, especially current affairs or niche channels (like
    Lifestyle or Discovery).

Do keyword research at http://www.wordtracker.com, http://inventory.overture.com/
Find the highest KEI keyword.
Good keywords are Learn, tips, tutorial, instruction, buy, trianing

Look at forum headers on domz.org
Google for common headline, look at what people are asking.
