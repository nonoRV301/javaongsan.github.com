Island Wide Wireless Access

Dec 1st, 2006

From 1st December, everyone in Singapore can enjoy free wireless access at public areas island wide. There are now 900 hot spots to tap on and the number looks set to grow progressively to 5000 by September next year. This service will extend up 2009.

Voip Voice-over-Internet-Protocol is like to ride on this, cheap phone with Voice-over-Internet-Protocol services for student, oversea workers, businessman making long distances calls. Broadband and cheap laptop allows business transaction anywhere anytime, no longer will we need to go home and surf in the evening. Information, emails, messengers services accessible anytime.

The number of local on line might not increase much but the time definitely will increase. What need to happen now is good batteries for laptops.

The most popular are definitely social networking sites, blogs and anything thats interactive.
