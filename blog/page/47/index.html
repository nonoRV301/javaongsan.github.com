Techniques to promote your business on MySpace

Did you check out Myspace’s forum? Most popular topic is music follow by reglion. How about the classified? What sort of product have you decided? Once you have decide on your product, you need to create a porfile thats suit your products. There several points to note when creating your profile.

Identity
You creating an identity that synchronize with your product and not you. If you selling woman shoes then you better be female, or if your are selling ring tones then you better be an teenager. You not lying but consider this as an advertisement. Would buy from an 60 year old grandfather who selling antiques? Yes, the profile is consistent with your product.

Picture
Make sure your picture is consistent with your profile. Just take a little picture with your digital camera or whatever and then use ms paint or your picture editing software and put in your URL at the bottom. Have something in your picture that have your product identity.

Opts-in
Myspace profile is the only social networking site that allows HTML codes, you create an opt-in from with autoresponder like aweber. When someone comes to your MySpace website and then opts-in to your list, and receive information or newsletters from you about your product.
