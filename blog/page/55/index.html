Easy Way to Get One Way Backlinks From High PR Sites

Sep 15th, 2007 | Comments

I am sure you are aware that commenting on other blogs is a good way to get backlinks for your blog. The problem a blog can have high PR on its main page but a different or none as an individual page so once your comment move to archive, the page rank drops right away. What we go is look for archives post with high Page Ranks to comment on. Use the Page Rank Search Tool on SEOChat.com. This tool list out individual page with their PR strength but they search general websites and cannot identified blogs separately. The trick will be to use “blogging” as a keyword in your search example “Making Money From Blogging” pulls up some archive posts from a few top bloggers. Work your way to the list and get 1000+ links in. On the next update you will have a higher PR for your blog.
