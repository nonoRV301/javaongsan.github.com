Race Report: FUJI XEROX Singapore Open Water Swimming 2006

Oct 9th, 2006 | Comments

Its a good day. The sky was clear and the weather was cool. I drove down to ECP at 11:20 my wave starts at 12:15. Once reached I went for body marking. Had to look around for the tent, the station are not really well placed and mark. Saw some my friends who had finish their 500m and 1500m swims.

About 12pm I went for my warm up swim. The current felt smooth and water was cool.
The race starts at 12:15 sharp. I was starting on the left for the first time, normally i go to the right. As expected I was caughtin the human jam right from the start. I started with freestlye move to the first boyle turn right everything felt ok. At the last turn heading towards shore, I start to drift away, the current was pushing me away, I end up a few 100m away from. I couldnt orientate using freestyle.

I switch to breaststroke for the 2nd and third round, I could feel myself slowing down. At the end of the third round my watch read 50min+. I couldnt believe it, the last time i did 1500m my timming was 34min. I had to put in some extra effort. I swam free style mix with breast for the 4th and 5th round.

On the 4th round I got stung by somthing, i didnt see what but it cause a sharp pain on my right shoulder. I was afriad I might get dizzy but nothing much happen so i continued. The last round I knew I would be the last there was only a 3 people running towards the sea. I slowly made my last round. Phew!

The result was out instantly, My timing was 1:47min. Not bad for a first timer. I shall focus more on my swim techniques once the marathon is over
